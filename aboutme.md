I am an **Executive Program Manager** and senior technology leader with a background in **Computer Engineering** and a Master’s degree from **La Sapienza University of Rome**, specialized in **Distributed Systems and Computer Architecture**.

I currently lead large-scale **modernization** and **digital transformation** programs in the **Welfare Public Administration** domain at **AlmavivA S.p.A.**, with end-to-end accountability for delivery, execution, and outcomes across a broad portfolio of initiatives. In this role, I coordinate cross-functional teams and multiple delivery streams, guiding Program Managers, Solution Architects, and Engineering teams while driving technology strategy, solution direction, and architectural evolution.

My experience combines strong program leadership with a solid technical foundation built over years of hands-on work on **distributed systems**, **microservices**, **cloud-native architectures**, and the transformation of complex legacy platforms into more scalable, efficient, and sustainable solutions.

Beyond program governance, I am deeply focused on innovation, engineering quality, and long-term sustainability of digital services. I am particularly interested in how architecture, platform strategy, and execution discipline can improve the way public services are designed and delivered.

Earlier in my career, I contributed to research in distributed and adaptive systems, publishing an **IEEE paper** presented at the **International Conference on Self-Adaptive and Self-Organizing Systems**. I also actively contribute to the technology community as a member of the **.NET Foundation**, **Developers Italia**, and the **Associazione Nazionale di Crittografia “De Componendis Cifris”**, and as a regular speaker at events such as **SharpCoding**, **Codemotion**, and **DotNetCode**.

Alongside my executive role, I maintain and contribute to several open-source projects focused on software architecture, interoperability, observability, and modern .NET engineering.

- **Technical blog:** [engineering87.github.io](https://engineering87.github.io)

My mission is to shape the future of digital public services through scalable, efficient, and sustainable technology solutions.
